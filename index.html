Our Story

Where "Atithi Devo Bhava" Comes to Life

Our Legacy

Founded in 1995 by the Sharma family, Shanti Dham began as a humble retreat for weary travelers seeking solace in the Himalayas. Today, it stands as a sanctuary where ancient Indian hospitality meets modern luxury.

The legacy of Shanti Dham is a testament to the power of ancient traditions to transcend boundaries and create a haven for those seeking a spiritual experience.

Our commitment to sustainability has been at the heart of our approach, ensuring that our resort is a haven for those who value eco-friendly practices.

Our Mission

To revive the timeless Indian principle of "Atithi Devo Bhava" (The Guest is God) through personalized care, sustainable practices, and immersive cultural experiences.

At Shanti Dham, we believe in the transformative power of ancient traditions to transcend boundaries and create a haven for those seeking a spiritual experience.

Our mission is to cultivate a sense of belonging and connection with nature, fostering a sense of harmony between man and nature.

Beyond Comfort, Into Culture

Where Every Moment Tells a Story

Spa & Wellness

Ayurvedic Rejuvenation

Detox with ancient Abhyanga massages and Shirodhara treatments by certified vaidyas, using herbs from our garden.

Sunrise Yoga

Greet the day with guided Hatha yoga sessions on our riverside deck, surrounded by Himalayan birdsong.

Cultural Programs

Kathak Under the Stars

Moonlit performances of classical Kathak dance, where artists narrate epics through rhythmic footwork and expressions.

Folk Music & Tales

Evenings by the bonfire with local artists singing Rajasthani ballads and tribal stories passed through generations.

Nature & Culinary

Guided Nature Walks

Explore hidden trails with naturalists to spot exotic birds, medicinal plants, and secret waterfalls.

Spice Kitchen Classes

Hands-on lessons to cook regional dishes in clay pots, using ingredients harvested from our organic farm.
